Link of Entities in Our Dataset with Others

Geographic Location

Field in the dataset: Geographic coordinates or locality names.

Potential links: You can link the geographic location of stations with other datasets containing socioeconomic or demographic data to analyze how different areas of Madrid are affected by pollution.

Additional Data Sources

Data from Spain's National Statistics Institute (INE) on demographic characteristics of Madrid (population density, income, etc.). Geospatial information about land use (residential, industrial, commercial) to study patterns in urban areas.

Example of Linkage

  • Monitoring Stations + Meteorological Conditions: Link the monitoring station with weather data from the same location to study how wind speed or humidity affects pollution levels in that area.
  • Date + Traffic Events: Analyze pollution data by days and hours and link it to traffic events in Madrid to see how pollution spikes coincide with peak traffic hours.
