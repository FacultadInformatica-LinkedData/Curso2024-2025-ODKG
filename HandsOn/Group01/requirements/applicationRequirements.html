About BiciMAP

BiciMAP is a user-friendly app designed to analyze and visualize bicycle data from the BiciMAD system in Madrid. The app allows users to explore key insights, such as the most visited routes, popular bicycle stations, and the relationship between specific areas of the city and overall transportation patterns.

Application User Interface

  • Dashboard Overview: Offers a comprehensive summary of metrics such as the total number of rides, top routes, busiest stations, and peak usage times.
  • Interactive Map: A core feature, this map shows bicycle routes, popular BiciMAD stations, and hotspots throughout Madrid. Users can explore city-wide patterns or zoom in on specific areas to analyze bicycle traffic.
  • Data Analysis Panel: Provides tools for filtering and comparing different datasets, allowing users to observe relationships between certain areas and how they connect to transportation options, such as public transit and cycling routes.
  • Route Statistics: Detailed statistics for selected routes, showing usage frequency, average duration, and other useful information for both casual cyclists and urban planners.
  • Customizable Reports: Users can generate custom reports based on the data, helping to identify trends, most popular routes, or areas of high demand.
