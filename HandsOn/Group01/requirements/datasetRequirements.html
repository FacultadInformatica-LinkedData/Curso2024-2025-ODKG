Dataset Requirements

1. License Identification

  • The data is copyrighted under Copyright: 2024 Mobility Labs, EMT Madrid, with the following license: Terms of Use
  • The dataset can be reused for both commercial and non-commercial purposes.
  • The information must not be manipulated or misrepresented in any way.
  • The use of the dataset should not harm the public image of EMT Madrid or be associated with illegal activities.
  • Any use of the data must include attribution, with the phrase: "Powered by EMT de Madrid" and a link to the EMT Madrid website (www.emtmadrid.es).
  • The use of dynamic data requires a unique access identifier, which must be requested from EMT Madrid through their form at this link.
