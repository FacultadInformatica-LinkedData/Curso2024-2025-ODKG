Description

Briefly:

This web application will facilitate the filtering and available information of districts, making it easy for anyone to find a neigboorhood based on its average price of living, energy efficiency and population. The application will also visualize the district's population, energy efficiency, and average price of living as well as other insights.

Features:

1. Filter based on prices of living in the neigboorhoods , energy efficiency and population: Users can enter their preferred values for any of the mentioned criteria and get matching results of neighborhoods vizulized in a map.

2. Information: By clicking on a neigboorhoods, users can see all the available information about it, such as location, energy efficiency, population and other usefull information collected from different data sources as well as data retrieved directly from wikidata.

3. Visualization: Users can follow links on that page that lead directly to wikidata entries.
