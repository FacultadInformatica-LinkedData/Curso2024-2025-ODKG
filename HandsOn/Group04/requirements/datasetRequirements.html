Dataset Requirements Satisfaction

R1: Smart Cities Domain

The dataset belongs to the smart cities domain as it provides data related to electric vehicle charging stations within Madrid. This is essential for urban planning, sustainability, and mobility in the context of smart city infrastructure.

R2: Data Available as CSV

The dataset is available in CSV format, fulfilling the requirement of being easy to process and analyze.
