Application Overview

Overview

This application is designed to improve the safety of car travel. Using a dataset of traffic accidents in the city of Madrid, it identifies the most dangerous roads and intersections, helping users avoid them. The application targets both human drivers and autonomous vehicles, providing valuable data to guide route choice for safer travel.

Key Points

  • Extract information from the dataset to discover potentially dangerous roads from the history of traffic accidents.
  • Help users or self-driving car algorithms plan the safest routes.
  • Utilize open data resources that are free and openly licensed.

Interface and Functionality

We will develop a user-friendly interface, similar to popular navigation services like Google Maps, to ensure effortless use. Additionally, the application will feature a REST API, enabling integration with other software and making it accessible to algorithms used by self-driving cars.
